New Engineer Onboarding

Getting started guide for engineers joining the MCSfMSC team

Week 1: Orientation

  • Complete HR onboarding and compliance training
  • Set up development environment and access permissions
  • Review MCSfMSC Portal (this site!) and all documentation
  • Meet with team lead and assigned buddy
  • Shadow 2-3 customer calls with senior engineers
  • Complete Microsoft Security certifications path review

Week 2: Product Deep-Dives

  • Complete Microsoft Sentinel hands-on lab
  • Complete Microsoft Defender XDR hands-on lab
  • Review all MCS Playbooks
  • Study Issue Resolution processes and escalation paths
  • Practice KQL queries in demo environment
  • Review recent customer engagement case studies

Week 3-4: Supervised Delivery

  • Co-lead customer calls with senior engineer oversight
  • Perform first customer configuration task (supervised)
  • Write first customer deliverable using delivery templates
  • Complete customer onboarding simulation exercise
  • Get sign-off from team lead for independent customer work
  • Present 30-day learning summary to team
